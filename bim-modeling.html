What We Offer in BIM Modeling

We provide comprehensive BIM services using Revit, Navisworks, and Tekla to create intelligent 3D models that improve collaboration, reduce errors, and streamline construction.

Architectural BIM Modeling

Detailed 3D models with walls, floors, roofs, doors, windows, and finishes.

Structural BIM Modeling

Steel, concrete, and timber structures with connections and reinforcement details.

MEP BIM Modeling

Mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and fire protection systems modeling.

Clash Detection & Coordination

Multi-discipline clash resolution using Navisworks for conflict-free construction.

4D Scheduling & Construction Sequencing

Time-based simulation linking model elements to project schedule.

5D Cost Estimation & Quantity Takeoff

Accurate material quantities and cost integration for budget control.

As-Built & Scan to BIM

Point cloud to BIM conversion for existing building documentation.

Facility Management Models (LOD 500)
